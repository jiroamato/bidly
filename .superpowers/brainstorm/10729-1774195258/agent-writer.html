Project Understanding AI Draft

We understand the City requires the replacement of approximately 2.4 kilometres of aging cast-iron water main with PVC pipe along Dundas Street West between Ossington Avenue and Dufferin Street. The scope encompasses full excavation, pipe installation, service reconnections to approximately 180 residential and commercial properties, complete road restoration to City standards, and comprehensive traffic management throughout the construction period.

We recognize the critical importance of maintaining water service to residents and businesses during construction and will implement a phased approach with temporary bypass connections to ensure zero service interruptions.
